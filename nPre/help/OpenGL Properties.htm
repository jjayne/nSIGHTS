OpenGL Properties

Open GL Properties control the use of anti-aliased lines (which may or may not be supported on your graphics card and selected visual). Anti-aliased lines blend pixels so that lines appear smooth. Anti-aliased lines typically are slower to draw than normal lines.

Anti-alias 2D lines If selected, lines on 2D plots are smoothed.
Anti-alias 3D lines If selected, lines on 3D plots are smoothed.
